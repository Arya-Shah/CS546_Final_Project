MyGarage

The most trusted websites to find reliable vehicle service center

Our application works with the motive of managing car maintenance, allowing users to find service centers based on service offered and ratings. Regular users are able to use our application to find the service centers that fit their needs, while service center owners are able to advertise and manage the services that they offer.

MyGarage Features

1. User can search and schedule an appointment with the best vehicle service center in the locality.
2. Ability to rate a garage and give reviews as well as choose favourites.
3. Garage Owners can manage appointments and track progress and earnings.
